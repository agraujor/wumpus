Game Instructions

Commands:
"l" or "L" : Player will turn left.
"r" or "R" : Player will turn right.
"g" or "G" : Player will walk.
"s" or "S" : Player will shoot an arrow which could kill Wumpus.
"e" or "E" : Player will try to exit if it is on the Exit.
"q" or "Q" : Quit the game.

Player starts at sector [0,n-1] toward EAST.
Game Parameters can be changed by re-assign constants in main.

Good luck!
